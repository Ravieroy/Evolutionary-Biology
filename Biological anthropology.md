**What did above mutation do?**
This mutation is responsible for enhancement of brain cell production and enlargement of neocortex(portion of brain vital for cognition). We know this because this gene was inserted into mice.

![[16f-human-GULO-gene.png]]

- The human GULO gene is a pseudogene, meaning it is non-functional due to mutations. [^1][^2][^3]
- The loss of GULO activity in humans occurred about 63 million years ago, during the divergence of the primate order into Haplorhini (which includes humans) and Strepsirrhini (which includes lemurs)(for details see [[Origin of Primates]])
- Due to this Humans cannot synthesize vitamin C and must obtain it from their diet. 
- ==The break in this gene is exactly in the same location as all the other Haplorhini primates.== This is an inherited trait for all the primates.

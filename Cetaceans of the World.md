*Skeleton of the early whale _Dorudon_. Notice the tiny hind limbs at left below the tail.*[^2]

Similar to land mammals cetaceans 
1. give birth to young ones
2. feed milk to their young
3. are warm blooded(extremely rare for a fish)
4. do not have gills but use fully developed lungs
5. have hairs(whiskers)
6. blowholes almost looking like a nostrils.(could be highly modified nasal holes)

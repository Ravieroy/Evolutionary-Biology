## Definitions
### Biological Anthropology
[[Biological anthropology]] is the study of human biology in an evolutionary framework. It aims to answer how did humans evolve from earlier hominids?
	Paleoanthropology : The search for and study of hominin fossils. 
	Primatology : The study of non-human primates.
